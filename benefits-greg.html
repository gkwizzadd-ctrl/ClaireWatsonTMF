Why This Matters To You

Four Problems, Solved in One Platform

Built and battle-tested on Claire's own portfolio — every feature below is live today, not a roadmap slide.

The Pain · Membership Churn
See renewal risk before it becomes a lost member
  • Live Low / Medium / High churn-risk scoring per account — built from real engagement signals, not guesswork
  • Renewal countdown alerts fire automatically inside a 90-day window, before it's a crisis
  • Auto-generated, specific next actions for every at-risk account — never a mystery what to do next
The Pain · Team Oversight
Know what every Engagement Manager is doing — without asking
  • Portfolio rolled up by Engagement Manager, Team and Region — filter to any slice in seconds
  • Real activity data — sessions, catalysts, pipeline, events — not self-reported status updates
  • Built to scale from one Engagement Manager today to a full team tomorrow, with zero re-architecture
The Pain · Executive Visibility
Give your Leadership Team confidence at a glance
  • One screen answers the four questions leadership always asks: accounts, at-risk count, renewals due, engagement level
  • Same live data your Engagement Managers use daily — nothing stale, nothing manually compiled for a meeting
  • A documented, consistent methodology behind every risk flag — a real answer when leadership asks "why is this account red?"
The Pain · Time
Give your team back the hours lost to status-chasing
  • Risk scores and next actions generate themselves from real activity — no more compiling weekly updates by hand
  • One login replaces a patchwork of spreadsheets, inboxes and calendars
  • An AI ODA Expert chatbot and gamified ODA Game handle first-line member engagement 24/7 — freeing your team for conversations that need a human
