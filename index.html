About Claire

Engagement Manager.
ODA advocate.
Industry connector.

As Engagement Manager at TM Forum, Claire Watson sits at the intersection of telecoms standards, digital transformation, and industry collaboration. She works with communications service providers around the world to help them understand, adopt, and derive real business value from the Open Digital Architecture.

With deep expertise across the ODA framework — from eTOM process design and SID information modelling to Open API implementation and AI-native canvas deployment — Claire translates the complexity of industry standards into practical, achievable transformation programmes.
