# TM Forum — Value Proposition for Prospecting

## What TM Forum Is
TM Forum is the global industry association for telecoms and digital services operators. It exists to help operators modernise, standardise, and automate their operations through Open Digital Architecture (ODA). Membership gives operators access to a global community, co-created assets, training, and proven frameworks that reduce cost, increase agility, and accelerate digital transformation.

## The Five ODA Industry Missions
These are the strategic pillars that TM Forum organises its work around. Every prospect conversation should map to one or more of these:

1. **Autonomous Networks** — AI-driven network operations; moving from manual to self-optimising, self-healing networks. Levels 1–5 maturity ladder. Operators targeting AN Level 3–4 are prime prospects.
2. **AI & Data** — Monetising AI across BSS/OSS; data platforms, AI factories, use-case acceleration. Operators building AI capabilities internally are highly relevant.
3. **Composable IT & Ecosystems** — Modular, cloud-native IT using ODA Components and Open APIs. Operators replacing monolithic stacks or launching B2B/API marketplaces.
4. **Business Assurance** — Revenue assurance, fraud management, and data quality. Operators under margin pressure or dealing with revenue leakage.
5. **Business Process / eTOM** — Standardised business process frameworks. Operators undertaking large-scale process transformation or ERP integration.

## Key TM Forum Assets (what members get)
- **Open APIs** — 50+ standardised APIs; operators use these to integrate systems and reduce vendor lock-in. Vendors get certified against them.
- **ODA Canvas & Components** — The architectural blueprint for a cloud-native telco. Reference architecture for transformation programmes.
- **Catalyst Projects** — Industry-wide proof-of-concept projects, run at TM Forum events (DTW, Innovate). Operators join as Champions; vendors join to demonstrate solutions. Real business outcomes, not just demos.
- **ODA Maturity Self-Assessment** — Benchmarks where an operator sits on the ODA journey across all 6 domains. Claire's entry point for most new members.
- **TM Forum Academy** — Formal training and certification (ODA Fundamentals, Open API practitioner, Autonomous Networks). Upskills teams.
- **SODA (Scaling ODA)** — Community initiative to drive real-world ODA deployment. Working groups, peer networking.
- **DTW (Digital Transformation World)** — Flagship annual event (Nice, June). Speaking and networking for senior members.
- **Innovate events** — Regional (Americas, Asia, Africa). More accessible entry points for prospect conversations.

## Why Operators Join TM Forum
- Reduce time-to-market by adopting proven standards rather than inventing from scratch
- Lower integration costs through Open API adoption
- Access peer knowledge from operators at the same stage of transformation
- Credibility and benchmarking through ODA conformance certification
- Access to vendor ecosystem — find the right partners through TM Forum's network
- Senior leadership visibility at DTW and Innovate events
- Regulatory and ESG reporting frameworks
