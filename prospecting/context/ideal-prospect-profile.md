# Ideal TM Forum Prospect Profile

## What We're Looking For
Telecoms or satellite operators in EMEA / Africa / Indian Ocean that are:
1. Not yet TM Forum members (or lapsed members)
2. Undergoing digital transformation — network modernisation, cloud migration, AI/data initiatives
3. Large enough to benefit from standards-based frameworks (typically 1M+ subscribers or significant B2B revenue)
4. Have a visible technology leadership team (CTO, CIO, Head of IT/Networks, CDO)

## Scoring Signals — High Priority (score 3 pts each)
- Active AI or automation programme (AI factory, ML ops, autonomous network pilots)
- Cloud-native or BSS/OSS transformation underway (replacing legacy stacks)
- Multi-OPCO group (standardisation across subsidiaries = strong ODA case)
- 5G rollout in progress or planned (AN missions highly relevant)
- Publicly stated digital transformation strategy
- Attending or presenting at industry events (MWC, AfricaCom, DTW)
- Vendor partnerships with ODA-aligned vendors (Ericsson, Nokia, Huawei, Amdocs, Netcracker)

## Scoring Signals — Medium Priority (score 2 pts each)
- Revenue growth or market expansion signals (new geographies, new services)
- ESG commitments (TM Forum has energy/sustainability frameworks)
- B2B or API monetisation ambitions
- Recent M&A or ownership change (new leadership = open to new frameworks)
- Regulatory compliance pressure (digital identity, data sovereignty)

## Scoring Signals — Lower Priority (score 1 pt each)
- General "digital transformation" language without specifics
- Attending industry events as delegates (not speakers)
- Vendor relationships without ODA alignment

## Red Flags (disqualifiers)
- Pure infrastructure/wholesale operator with no retail or B2B digital services
- State-owned operator with no transformation mandate or budget
- Market too small (<500k subscribers, no growth trajectory)
- Already a TM Forum member (check membership list)

## Reference Members (use as benchmarks)
**Axian Group** (AXN001) — Africa & Indian Ocean, 10 OPCOs, Telco/Fintech/Energy. Multi-OPCO group with strong transformation mandate. Jerome Albou (Group CTIO) as Champion. Entry via AN and AI & Data missions.

**Maroc Telecom** (MTL001) — Africa & MENA, Morocco-based, Telecommunications. CTO-driven membership. Main focus AN and AI for networks. AI factory with multiple use cases. Roadmap to AN Level 4.

## Ideal First Conversation Opener
The ODA Maturity Self-Assessment — a no-commitment benchmarking exercise that shows the operator where they sit vs. peers. Non-threatening, high-value, creates a natural follow-up.
