A Closer Look

What Each Phase Means For TM Forum

Phase 1 · Live Today
A complete engagement operating system
Every capability in this phase is already built and in active use — not a concept, a working platform. It gives Engagement Managers a single home for relationship-building, prospecting and account intelligence, and gives leadership real-time visibility into portfolio health without waiting for a manually compiled report.
Phase 2 · Coming Next
Built for a growing team
As the Engagement Management function grows beyond a single person, these enhancements make the platform work naturally across teams and regions — richer reporting, smoother navigation between views, and a faster ramp for every new Engagement Manager who joins.
Phase 3 · Future Vision
Ready to scale with TM Forum
The strategic horizon: connecting Engagement Intelligence with the systems TM Forum already relies on, layering in AI-powered search across the full body of engagement and ODA knowledge, and an architecture designed from day one to grow from a handful of accounts to the entire membership base.

This roadmap isn't a wish list — Phase 1 is already live. Every future phase builds on a platform that's already proving its value, one account at a time.
